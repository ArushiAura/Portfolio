About Me

I am a materials chemist and electrochemical researcher with a strong foundation in sustainable energy technologies and circular resource utilization. I obtained my Ph.D. in Material Science at the Institute of Nano Science and Technology (INST) and Indian Institue of Science Education and Research (IISER) Mohali, with a perfect academic record. My doctoral work focused on surface-engineered metal sulphides for efficient electrochemical hydrogen production. My expertise spans water splitting, electrocatalysis, battery electrode materials, and nanostructured material synthesis and characterization. I have contributed to 20+ research publications, multiple patents, and authored book chapters in green and sustainable technologies. I bring hands-on experience with advanced techniques like XPS, TEM, BET, and electrochemical workstations, and have actively engaged in knowledge dissemination through conferences and collaborative projects. Driven by a passion for solving environmental challenges through material innovation, I aim to continue contributing to sustainable and renewable energy technologies through research and applied science.
